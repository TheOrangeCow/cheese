About Cheese Factory

We’re a family-run dairy crafting artisan cheeses in the heart of the countryside since 1985. Every wheel is made by hand using local milk and traditional techniques, ensuring the finest taste and texture.

From our creamy cheddar to our adventurous seasonal blends, every bite supports sustainable farming and British craftsmanship.
